About the Project

Project Silhouette is a compelling collection of short films produced by the talented students of CPE32S3, aimed at raising awareness about the dangers of various cybercrimes prevalent in today's digital world. Each film portrays realistic scenarios that highlight how individuals can fall victim to threats such as phishing, identity theft, cyberbullying, online scams, and data breaches. Through engaging storytelling and thoughtful direction, the project seeks to educate viewers on the importance of cybersecurity, responsible internet use, and how to protect themselves in the ever-evolving online landscape.
